Inter-VDOM links
----------------
NP4 and NP6 processor include npu0_vlink and npu1_vlink for accelerated inter-VDOM communication


SSL VPN hardware acceleration
-----------------------------
CP8 and CP9 support SSL VPN HW acceleration through bulk data engine


Logging
-------
Traffic offloaded to NP6 and NP6Lite processors does not log traffic stats

Traffic offloaded to NP7 processors have improved logging of traffic stats


Antivirus
---------
FG models with SoC4, NP6 or NP7 can accelerate anitvirus processing (not proxy-based mode)

NP8 and NP9 support advanced cp-accel-mode aswell
